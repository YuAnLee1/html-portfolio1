The Best Movies According to Yu

My top 3 movies of all-time

Star Wars Episode 3 (Prequel)

Explains what ultimately drives Anakin down the path of the dark side.

Star Wars Episode 6 (Original)

Shows the start of the defeat of the Empire and presents the end of the Tragedy of Anakin Skywalker.

Tron : Legacy

The movie immerses us into the world of a videogame project created by the father of the protagonist.
